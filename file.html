                        What are the most popular exams for Dental courses in india?

                        AIIMS PG,JIPMER PG,NEET UG are some of the most popular exams for Dental courses in india.

                        What are the most popular specializations in Dental courses in india ?

                        Surgery ,Oral Pathology , Hygiene ,Prosthodontics ,Oral Medicine and Radiology ,Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery ,CONSERVATIVE DENTISTRY AND ENDODONTICS are amongst the most popular specializations for Dental courses in india.

                        Which cities near india also offer Dental related courses ?
